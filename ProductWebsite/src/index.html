Explorator

Is an integrated, time based healthcare optimisation and care pathway process improvement platform. It helps manage patients through their care pathway from referral to treatment and discharge so that a Trust can meet their 18 Week and Cancer RTT targets.

Learn more »

It's real-time.

So that healthcare professionals have access to up to date data and know exactly where their patients are in their care pathway.

Explorator integrates to hospital administration systems to receive patient demographics, referrals, Pathway ID, Clock Start/Stop dates and pathway events such as booking appointments, scheduled admission date.

It also integrates with Order Comms and Results Reporting systems to identify orders placed and result available dates.

It’s intuitive.

You get the information you need on your dashboard, or through easy to use menus to quickly see potential pressures and future RTT breaches so that you can drill down and take immediate action.

It's proactive.

It incorporates key activity patient flow, with event milestone targets and actual performance dates with pre and post alert triggers to enable positive action to be taken to manage patients to prevent RTT breaches.

The alerts ensure that clinicians, managers and admin staff can proactively intervene and manage patients through their pathway before their journey becomes problematic.

It’s customisable to your needs.

Explorator allows you to set you own event milestone durations (days) which can be specialty specific. Local access policy management arrangements can be incorporated, for example to help DNA and multiple appointment cancelation referrals back to primary care.

It offers powerful reports.

Explorator has full reporting capability to summarise patient activity within pathways of care, and show RTT compliance and breaches for admitted, non-admitted care and incomplete pathways at Trust, Hospital, Specialty and Consultant levels.

Instead of reporting failure you will be reporting success!

It reduces cost and improves efficiency and increases productivity and performance.

Proactive management of patients through their care pathways will:

  • Reduce excessive management overheads
  • Reduce staff workloads
  • Improve waiting time management and avoid RTT breaches
  • Avoid potential penalties or loss of income transferring patients to other Trusts for treatment to resolve excessive breaching and performance issues
  • Improve patient experience and Trust reputation

Explorator helps Trusts to transform their RTT management and reporting processes, and to identify bottlenecks and delays in care pathway flow so that positive action can be taken and changes made.
